Ensure Radio Coverage For Our First Responders

The Florida Fire Prevention Code 8th Edition brings awareness to the importance of in-building radio communications coverage surveys or assessments. These surveys evaluate the coverage of First Responder radio communications inside buildings to ensure that first responders can communicate effectively in an emergency.
