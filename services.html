Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is an ERRCS System

    An ERRCS (Emergency Responder Radio Communication System) is a Public Safety communications infrastructure that ensures seamless radio coverage for First Responders in buildings.

  • What is ERRCS Testing

    ERRCS testing involves conducting a survey to map the in-building signal strength of Public Safety radio systems. The results of this survey are typically overlaid on your building floor plans, highlighting local deficiencies in the in-building radio signal strength.
